User Research

We went to ralphs to learn about how people grocery shop. In the image below, Vincent is consulting his weekly ads, his shopping list, and his coupons while he shops and shuffles through each.

Brainstorming

After our user research, we concluded that shopping takes a lot of repetitive work like checking for the items on the shelves multiple times, double backing for items they already have, and going back and forth between grocery stores.

Storyboarding

In answer to the question, "How can we aid people's decision making process when grocery shopping?" We created 3 storyboard to portray 3 different ideas.
